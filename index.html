Our mission

The world is in panic and our lives
are uncertain, but in these times, poetry
offers a solace. Steady meters and lulling rhymes attempt
to help you to lose yourself in an emotion, in a feeling and to just leave the chaos of
the current day for just a moment and to immerse yourself in
another realm.
